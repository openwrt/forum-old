FriedZombie

Thnx danymarc, but sometimes people can be wrong thoug. Also nebbia88 knows a lot as well

Yeah since it is a 4mb router, luci will not fit in the jffs2 partition. In that case it has to be included in the image before flashing
There are two options to do this, via the image builder or via buildroot.

http://wiki.openwrt.org/doc/howto/obtai … e.generate

But I prepared an image you could flash to your device including luci via the imagebuilder.
https://drive.google.com/file/d/0B5n3Fy … sp=sharing
download and copy it via scp to your router (winscp is a good client on windows).
store it into the /tmp directory
after that is done, go into the console on the router and type sysupgrade /tmp/openwrt-ar71xx-generic-tl-wr741nd-v2-squashfs-sysupgrade.bin
After this is the router is flashed with a firmware that includes a webui.
