Post #3
stas2z
19 Nov 2015, 19:26

upd, 99% your error is cuz both of your client and ap networks binded to lan interface (client can't be binded to bridge)

as 841 using original firmware you can't use wds, so you need to bind ap to lan, sta to other network (wwan as example) and setup relayd pseudobridge

or install openwrt to 841 and use wds access point and wds client mode to create a wireless bridge
