Hi,
I flashed OpenWRT (Backfire 10.03.1-RC6) at my TL-WR1043ND and attached a Logitech USB-Webcam. It runs fine using "mjpeg-streamer" and the was no trouble to stream pictures by the routers webserver (http://192.168.2.1:8080/?action=stream). Alternatively I tried to caputre images by "motion" on the router. It run fine too - of course since routers memory was exceeded (about 20 captures). So next step was to attach an USB-Stick additionally by an USB-Hub and I installed "kmod-usb-storage", "kmod-usb-ext4" etc. But after installing "block-mount" and "block-hotplug" data-access from USB-Webcam went crazy, lots of corrupted images (> 90%). Then I removed  "block-mount" and "block-hotplug" and rebooted the rooter and the webcam was fine again. Installing  "block-mount" and "block-hotplug" and images were corrupted again.
Do I need  "block-mount" and "block-hotplug"  to mount USB-Stick?
