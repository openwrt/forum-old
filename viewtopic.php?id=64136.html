Post #9
wrtboy
19 Apr 2016, 00:27

try this after you putty into your router.

execute this command.  you should have wireless turned on now.

uci set wireless.@wifi-device[0].disabled=0; uci commit wireless; wifi

connect the router to your modem.  you should see a ssid of openwrt.  connect to it.

putty in again via wireless and execute these commands.

opkg update
opkg install luci-ssl
reboot

you should be able to reach LuCI at 192.168.1.1

wrtboy
19 Apr 2016, 19:18

you cannot have two devices sharing the same ipv4 address.

your huawei's ipv4 address is 192.168.1.1

1.  change the tp-link ipv4 address to 192.168.10.1
2.  connect the tp-link to the huawei
3.  you should be able to reach tp-link's LuCI at 192.168.10.1
