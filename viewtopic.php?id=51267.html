Looks like your router has US regulatory rules burned into the memory. TP-Link has a habit of doing this out of lazyness, and overriding it in software. Openwrt won't allow override in software and obeys only the hardware unless you custom compile the driver.

Not sure if reghack still works. Someone recently stated it got broken by a recent update, not sure if it was made to work again afterwards.
